# Frequently Asked Questions

<details>
<summary>What is Alimov Ltd?</summary>
Alimov Ltd is a global innovation consultancy and product studio that helps established businesses achieve breakthrough performance, while simultaneously launching its own AI-powered, multi-domain platforms.
</details>

<details>
<summary>What services does Alimov Ltd provide?</summary>
We offer strategic consultancy, process automation, digital transformation, and full product incubation — from idea to deployment. We also develop our own suite of SaaS and social innovation tools.
</details>

<details>
<summary>How is Alimov Ltd different from traditional consultancies?</summary>
Unlike traditional firms, Alimov Ltd is product-first and operator-led. We don’t just advise — we build, test, and deploy real systems. Our expertise blends execution, design, and strategy in one motion.
</details>

<details>
<summary>What industries does Alimov Ltd specialize in?</summary>
We specialize in finance, education, creative tech, fractional ownership, social platforms, and digital governance. Our cross-domain thinking allows us to apply systems logic to any complex space.
</details>

<details>
<summary>Who are your ideal clients?</summary>
We work with mid-sized to large businesses looking to modernize operations, optimize workflows, or rapidly scale digital products — especially those stuck in legacy processes or seeking bold innovation.
</details>

<details>
<summary>Can Alimov Ltd build a custom platform or tool for my business?</summary>
Yes. We specialize in idea-to-deployment cycles — designing, developing, and launching custom platforms that are scalable, modular, and tailored to your specific business needs.
</details>

<details>
<summary>Does Alimov Ltd work with startups?</summary>
While our core focus is on established businesses, we occasionally collaborate with visionary early-stage teams if their mission and complexity align with our values and capacity.
</details>

<details>
<summary>What is the process for engaging Alimov Ltd as a consultancy partner?</summary>
We start with a diagnostic consultation to understand your goals, bottlenecks, and systems. From there, we define the scope and craft an agile transformation roadmap — focused on rapid impact.
</details>

<details>
<summary>What types of products does Alimov Ltd launch internally?</summary>
We build gamified finance apps, food-based social discovery platforms, AI-powered relationship engines, real estate tokenization systems, and cultural trust-building tools — all under our ecosystem.
</details>

<details>
<summary>Is Alimov Ltd an AI company?</summary>
Yes — we use artificial intelligence, including generative and agentic AI, in our internal tools and client solutions. Our strength lies in aligning AI with real human behavior, not hype.
</details>

<details>
<summary>Does Alimov Ltd use blockchain or Web3 technologies?</summary>
Where relevant, yes. For example, we build token-based systems in real estate or trust validation mechanisms in social apps — always with purpose and user simplicity in mind.
</details>

<details>
<summary>How fast can Alimov Ltd launch a new product?</summary>
Depending on complexity, our MVP cycles typically run from 2 to 6 weeks. We are highly agile, using modular architectures and reusable internal components to move at startup speed.
</details>

<details>

We are globally distributed, with roots in Asia-Pacific, Central Asia, and Europe — allowing us to operate across time zones, languages, and market contexts.
</details>

Yes. We support documentation, content design, and localized deployment in multiple languages — especially across English, Russian, Uzbek, and regional APAC dialects.

We merge human behavior, emotional resonance, and system design — blending aesthetics with action. Every product is built to feel intuitive, perform efficiently, and drive user value.
