## 📉 What Is IRR (and Why It Doesn't Work for Dating)?

**IRR (Internal Rate of Return)** is used to measure the profitability of investments based on expected cash flows.

<div className="bg-gray-50 p-6 rounded-lg my-6">
  <h3 className="text-lg font-semibold mb-4">Traditional IRR Formula:</h3>
  <div className="bg-white p-4 rounded font-mono text-center text-lg">
    NPV = Σ (Cash Flow_t / (1 + IRR)^t) = 0
  </div>
</div>

But in dating:

<div className="grid md:grid-cols-2 gap-6 my-6">
  <div className="bg-red-50 p-4 rounded-lg border-l-4 border-red-400">
    <h4 className="font-semibold text-red-800 mb-2">❌ Traditional Finance</h4>
    <ul className="text-red-700 space-y-1">
      <li>• Predictable cash flows</li>
      <li>• Measurable ROI</li>
      <li>• Clear exit strategy</li>
      <li>• Risk assessment models</li>
    </ul>
  </div>
  <div className="bg-pink-50 p-4 rounded-lg border-l-4 border-pink-400">
    <h4 className="font-semibold text-pink-800 mb-2">💔 Dating Finance</h4>
    <ul className="text-pink-700 space-y-1">
      <li>• Cash flows = "good vibes"</li>
      <li>• Initial investment = emotions + $37 cocktails</li>
      <li>• Exit strategy = ????</li>
      <li>• Risk = existential dread</li>
    </ul>
  </div>
</div>

So the formula breaks down faster than your 4th Hinge date in a row.
