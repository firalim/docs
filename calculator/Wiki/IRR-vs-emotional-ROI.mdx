## 📉 What Is IRR (and Why It Doesn't Work for Dating)?

<Tabs>
  <Tab title="Traditional IRR">
    **IRR (Internal Rate of Return)** measures investment profitability based on expected cash flows.

    ```mathematical formula
    NPV = Σ (Cash Flow_t / (1 + IRR)^t) = 0
    ```

    <CardGroup cols={2}>
      <Card title="✅ Traditional Finance" icon="bank" color="#10b981">

        - Risk assessment models
