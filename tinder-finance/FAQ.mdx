### How does Budget Battle Royale work?

**Setup**: You start with a random monthly income ($2,500-$5,000)

**Gameplay**:

1. **Drag & Drop Budget**: Allocate funds to:
   - 🏠 Essentials (rent, groceries, utilities)
   - 🎉 Fun (entertainment, dining, hobbies)
   - 💰 Savings & Investing
2. **Dodge Financial Traps**:
   - "Car repair: -\$400"
   - "Forgotten subscription: -\$50"
   - "Friend's wedding gift: -\$200"
3. **Make Quick Decisions**:
   - "Friend's Vegas trip: Join (-\$600) or Skip?"
   - "Crypto bro pitches NFT: Invest (-\$200) or Pass?"

**Results**:

- ✅ "You survived with \$50 left\! 🏆"
- ❌ "Bankrupt by Day 20 😵"

**XP Rewards**: 200 XP for surviving, 50 XP per trap dodged, 100 XP for multiplayer wins

### How does the Financial Roast Generator work?

**Input Process**:

1. Select 3-5 financial blunders from common scenarios
2. AI analyzes your choices and spending patterns
3. Generates personalized roast content

**Example Output**:

> _"Wow, you spent \$300 on sneakers? Your bank account called—it's walking barefoot now. 😬"_

**Features**:

- 💡 **Redemption Tips**: Actionable advice following each roast
- 🗳️ **Community Voting**: Rate roasts with 😂 Brutal, 🩹 Ouch, 💡 Wise
- 📱 **Shareable Content**: Export as videos for social media

**XP Rewards**: 75 XP for submissions, 50 XP for upvotes, 100 XP for shares
